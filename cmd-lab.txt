# b- calculate the mean seasonal cycle for the period 1981-2010 over your_country

cdo selyear,1981/2010 congo_precip.nc congo_1981_2010.nc
cdo ymonmean congo_1981_2010.nc congo_seasonal_cycle.nc

cdo selyear,1981/2010  -selmon,05,06,07,08 congo_precip.nc  congo_dry_season_1981_2010.nc
cdo ymonmean congo_dry_season_1981_2010.nc congo_mean_dry.nc
